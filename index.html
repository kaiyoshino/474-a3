Investment Portfolio

This visualization displays investment assets over time as a portfolio. Portfolio visualizations are a fundamental tool in investing. They allow users to get a broad look at the diversification and value of their investments in real time. The blue line in the line chart represents the value of the portflio. Click any asset in the doughnut chart to see its value over time. You can zoom in one a given time by sliding and dragging the grey bar below the line chart or scrolling over the line chart
